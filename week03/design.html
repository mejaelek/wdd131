Proximity

Related items are grouped together to enhance readability. This principle helps users quickly associate content.

Alignment

All elements are visually connected through alignment. This ensures consistency and order in the layout.

Repetition

Consistent styling (colors, fonts, spacing) throughout the site reinforces the visual identity and usability.
